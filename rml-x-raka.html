About the Collection

This collection blends Raka Studio's flair for artisanal, sculptural forms with Courtney's colorful, whimsical style — resulting in pieces that are both functional and fantastical.

The designs channel the surreal charm of Alice in Wonderland into five striking pieces of collectible furniture and lighting: a daybed, vanity chair, wall console, mirror, and light fixture.

It is always elegant, but with a wink and a smile.

Raka
Studio

Lahore, Pakistan

Raka is built by three brothers. Everything is made by hand using a stack lamination technique developed over years of making — thin strips of wood stacked, glued, ground, and hand-sanded into form. The studio makes furniture, lighting, and objects for clients internationally.
