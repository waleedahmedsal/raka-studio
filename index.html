About
Raka Studio

The Studio is three brothers — Saleh, Waleed, and Rayan — and their self-taught skill to design and produce themselves. Their first pieces were bent to existence at their family home.

Inspired by the flows of nature and driven by their passion for discovering the possible variance in design; they create. The keynote in their creative concept is to dispel the prevalent discipline of design.

They design and then bind their pieces themselves by hand — putting in days and weeks to put together each work of their functional art. Not limited by the formalities of education in the field; they have experimented their way into creating their artistic pieces, allowing them to branch beyond the contemporary.
